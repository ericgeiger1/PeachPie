🧮 Understanding Modulus (%)
Modulus gives the remainder.

Example:

23 ÷ 5 = 4 pies
23 % 5 = 3 leftover peaches

If remainder = 0 → it is a multiple of 5
If remainder = 1–4 → not a multiple of 5

You do NOT need an if statement.
Just print the remainder and interpret it.
